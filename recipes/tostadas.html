Tostadas

Tostadas are just great. The elaboration barely requires much time and the final result wont let you down.

This is a variant from the traditional toasts in Mexico, but they are very common near coasts and beaches.

The addition of shrimps make a very tasteful bite, accompanied with beans and jicama all together with the crunchy toast, they bring a mixture of consistency that will get you pleased and extasied. Learn to prepare them now!

Ingredients (2 people)

  • 1 tablespoon soybean oil
  • 1/4 onions
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 6 tomatoes
  • 4 morita peppers
  • 1/4 cups of peanut
  • 3/4 cups mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups of shrimp
  • 8 toasts
  • refried beans
  • enough of jicama

Steps

  1. In a deep skillet, pour the soybean oil and cook the onion, garlic, tomato, morita pepper and peanut for 10 minutes. Then blend everything with the mayonnaise.
  2. In a skillet, add the soybean oil and cook the garlic, shrimp and previously blended mixture for 15 minutes, or until the shrimp are cooked.
  3. Spread the toast with the beans, add the shrimp, jicama and parsley.
  4. Accompany with lemons and enjoy.
